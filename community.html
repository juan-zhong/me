Community program for students from underdeveloped counties 县城学子互助计划

In 2020, I collebrated with a group of friends from Anyuan and initiated a community-based program to share our experiences and perspectives with younger Anyuan people in high schools and colleges. The vision of this program is to foster a community where peers from underdeveloped towns in China help each other. We run a mentoring for growth plan (CTHH) twice a year (every summer and winter break) and a drop-in chatting room (OHOH) weekly during fall semesters.

In CTHH, people voluntarily sign up as mentors and mentees and are grouped into pairs based on shared backgrounds and interests. Each pair has a 60-90 minute online conversation where the mentor shares their individual experiences and stories with the mentee who have questions regarding study, work, or life.

In OHOH, members from the CTHH plan freely join in the weekly online chatting room hosted by a regular mentor and have conversations about their questionsa and interests.

By 2025, we have run 11 cycles of CTHH (2020-2025) and three cycles of OHOH (2022-2025), In addition, We also organized two special summer gatherings (2022 & 2023) where we had theme-based dicussions by community members and talks by invited guests.
