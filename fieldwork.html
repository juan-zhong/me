Anyuan Hakka

Born and raised in Anyuan, China, I speak Anyuan Hakka, a lesser-studied variety of the Hakka language. Since 2021, I have initiated an ongoing documentation project, in which I collaborate with local community members to document, analyze, preserve, and promote Anyuan Hakka.

In 2024, I developed an introductory course of general linguistics and taught it in Anyuan Hakka to a group of community members who have no prior linguistics background. The course provided easy access to linguistic knowledge for local community members who do not speak English (or Mandarin) and equipped them with basic lingusitic tools to better understand Anyuan Hakka, their own everyday language.

I also run an online Anyuan Hakka chatting group with young generations from Anyuan. The chatting group meets online every week, aiming at creating a vibrant space for people who are from Anyuan but have (im)migrated to other places to continuously speak Anyuan Hakka and maintain their proficiency.
